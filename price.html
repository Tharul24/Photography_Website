FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

01
How would you describe your approach to capturing moments?

I focus on capturing genuine emotions and authentic moments. I believe in building a rapport with my clients to create a relaxed atmosphere that allows their personalities to shine through in the photographs.

02
Do you specialize in any particular type of photography (e.g., newborn, family, commercial)?

While I enjoy capturing a variety of subjects, I specialize in weddings, fashion, and models. However, I am open to new challenges and love exploring different photographic styles.

03
What is your policy on image copyright and usage rights?

You retain the copyright to all photographs. However, I retain the right to use selected images for promotional purposes, such as my website or social media. I will always obtain your permission before using any specific images.

04
Do you offer photo albums or prints as additional options?

Yes, I offer a range of high-quality photo albums and prints in various sizes and styles. I can also create custom-designed products to match your preferences.

05
Do you provide digital files in high resolution?

Yes, all delivered photos will be in high resolution format, allowing you to print them in any size without compromising image quality.

06
How far in advance should I book your services?

I recommend booking as early as possible to secure your preferred date, especially for weddings and significant events. Ideally, booking a few months in advance is best, but I may be able to accommodate last-minute requests depending on availability.
